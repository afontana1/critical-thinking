# Critical Questions for Religious Studies & Critical Thinking

## Purpose of this Question Bank

These questions are designed to support the course **Religious Studies & Critical Thinking**. They are meant to help students think carefully about religion as lived practice, worldview, institution, symbolic system, historical phenomenon, truth-claim, and object of academic inquiry.

The questions are intended for seminar discussion, writing prompts, research projects, debate preparation, source analysis, capstone work, and self-reflection. They aim to cultivate a stance that is neither credulous nor dismissive: methodologically pluralist, historically sensitive, evidentially calibrated, interpretively charitable, and argumentatively disciplined.

---
